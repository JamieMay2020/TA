The Twelve Steps of Trenchers Anonymous

1. We admitted we were powerless over our trading compulsions—that our financial lives had become unmanageable.
2. Came to believe that a Power greater than ourselves could restore us to financial sanity.
3. Made a decision to turn our financial decisions and trading habits over to the care of sound principles and professional guidance.
4. Made a searching and fearless moral inventory of our trading history and financial behaviors.
5. Admitted to ourselves, to our Higher Power, and to another human being the exact nature of our compulsive trading.
6. Were entirely ready to have our Higher Power remove all these defects of character that led to poor financial decisions.
"Grant me the serenity to accept the market conditions I cannot control,
The courage to change the trading behaviors I can,
And the wisdom to know the difference."
